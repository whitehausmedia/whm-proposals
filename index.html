Search Engine Optimization

Get found by the people already searching for what you do.

Technical SEO Audits

We crawl your entire site and identify every issue hurting your rankings — broken links, slow pages, missing meta tags, indexing problems, Core Web Vitals failures. You get a prioritized fix list and we handle the implementation.

On-Page Optimization

Every page gets optimized: title tags, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, image alt text, internal linking structure, schema markup, and keyword placement. We write the copy changes, implement them, and monitor the ranking impact.

Keyword Strategy & Content

We research what your ideal customers are actually typing into Google, then build content around those exact searches — blog posts, service pages, location pages, FAQ sections. Each piece is written, published, and tracked for performance.

Local SEO & Map Pack

Google Business Profile fully optimized, local citations built across 50+ directories, review generation strategy deployed, and location-specific content created. We get you showing up in the map pack where buying decisions happen.

Monthly Reporting & Monitoring

Ranking dashboards, traffic analytics, competitor tracking, and Search Console monitoring — delivered monthly with plain-English analysis. You always know what's working, what changed, and what we're doing about it.

Custom Shopify Storefronts

Your Shopify backend. Our custom-designed frontend. No theme limitations.

Why This Matters

Most Shopify stores look the same because they're built on the same 20 themes. Yours doesn't have to. We build a completely custom storefront that connects to your Shopify backend — so you keep everything that works (inventory, checkout, payments, orders) while getting a frontend that looks and performs like nothing your competitors have.

Blazing Fast Performance

Standard Shopify themes load in 3-5 seconds. Our custom storefronts load in under 1 second. Pages are pre-built and served from edge servers closest to your customer. Faster pages mean lower bounce rates, higher conversions, and better Google rankings.

Total Design Freedom

No more working around theme limitations. Custom product pages, unique collection layouts, branded checkout flows, interactive product configurators, lookbooks, size guides — whatever your brand needs, we build it. Your store looks like a $50,000 custom build, not a $200 template.

You Still Use Shopify

Nothing changes on your end. You still manage products, inventory, orders, and fulfillment from the same Shopify dashboard you already know. We just replace the part your customers see with something dramatically better. All your existing apps and integrations keep working.

Built to Scale

Flash sales that crash your theme? Not anymore. Custom storefronts handle traffic spikes without breaking a sweat. And when you're ready to expand — mobile app, in-store kiosk, wholesale portal — the same backend powers all of it.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is included with every website?

+

Every site ships with SEO configuration, GA4 analytics, performance optimization, mobile-first responsive design, and SSL security — all configured from day one. You also get a knowledge base so you can make edits yourself if you want to.

How long does a website build take?

+

Most projects launch within one week. We keep the process tight: a discovery call, site audit, custom build, and launch with analytics configured. Urgent projects (48-hour turnaround) available for rush fees.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

+

Yes. Our Site Care plans include hosting, security updates, backups, content edits, and monthly performance reporting. Start at $97/month for full support, or manage it yourself with our knowledge base access.

What if I need to update my site after launch?

+

With Site Care, we handle updates for you. Without it, we provide a knowledge base and video tutorials so you can make simple edits yourself. More complex changes are available à la carte.

Can you redesign my existing site?

+

Yes. We can do a full redesign, a refresh (keep the layout, modernize the design), or a rebuild from scratch. Most clients choose a full rebuild to get the best results. We'll audit your current site first to identify what's working.
