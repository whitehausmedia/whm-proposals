Where the opportunity lives

Your online presence has room to work harder for you.

After reviewing your current website, we identified four areas where a stronger digital presence would directly impact how many new families choose Bright Beginnings.

01

The Site Does Not Reflect a 5-Star Center

Your current website uses stock photography throughout — none of the images show your actual facility, classrooms, or staff. Parents in zip code 27519, where the median household income is $165,000, are comparing options carefully. A site built on generic stock images next to centers with polished, branded websites creates a disconnect with the quality of care you actually provide.

02

No Mobile-Responsive Design

The current site scores a 5 out of 100 on mobile usability. Parents searching "child care near me Cary" are overwhelmingly on their phones. When a site does not render properly on mobile, most visitors leave within seconds — before they ever learn about your Creative Curriculum, your ratios, or your 26-year track record.

03

Your Story Is Not Being Told

Shirley's 32 years in Wake County schools, Leon's 38 years as a principal, the property built on Bertha Scott Herndon's land, Lisa and Linwood continuing the family mission — this is an extraordinary founding story. The current site mentions none of it. In a market full of franchise centers, your family legacy is your strongest differentiator and it is completely absent from your digital presence.

04

No Search Engine Foundation

The site has no meta descriptions, no Open Graph tags for social sharing, no LocalBusiness schema markup, and no structured data that helps Google understand what Bright Beginnings is. For competitive local searches in the Cary 27519 area, these fundamentals are what determine whether families find you or find a competitor first.
