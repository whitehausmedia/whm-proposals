Where the opportunity lives

Four things holding Smiles of Cary back online.

After reviewing your current site and digital footprint, here is what we found — and why it's costing you new patient inquiries every single week.

01

An Outdated Design That Signals the Wrong Thing

The current Smiles of Cary website has a visual design that is several years behind where patient expectations are today. Heavy gradients, tight columns, and small typography signal an older practice — not a modern, welcoming one. Design is trust. Patients make a split-second judgment about your practice the moment they land on your site. Right now, that first impression isn't serving you.

02

No Online Scheduling — Patients Choose the Easy Option

Cary families searching for a dentist in 2026 expect to book online. Your current site requires a phone call to schedule, which introduces friction at the exact moment a prospective patient is ready to commit. Practices with online booking convert at a significantly higher rate from search traffic. Without it, you're handing those patients to the practice down the road that made it easier.

03

A Poor Mobile Experience Losing Half Your Traffic

The majority of patients searching for a dentist in Cary do it from a phone — commuting, at school pickup, during lunch. The current site doesn't adapt cleanly to mobile screens: text runs too small, navigation is cramped, and the booking path is buried. A mobile-first rebuild ensures every patient who finds you on their phone has a seamless, confidence-building experience.

04

No Patient Reviews Visible on the Site

Patient reviews are the single most persuasive trust signal for a dental practice. If someone is comparing you to another dentist and only one site shows glowing testimonials from real patients in Cary, that's the one they call. Your current site has no reviews section. Building one directly into the site — with real quotes, real names, real locations — closes that gap immediately.
