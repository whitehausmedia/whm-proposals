What We Offer

Complete care for every smile in your family.

General checkups to full smile makeovers — we handle it all under one roof, with the same warm team every visit.

General Dentistry

Cleanings, exams, fillings, and X-rays. Preventive care that keeps your whole family healthy and cavity-free year after year.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Professional whitening, porcelain veneers, and Invisalign clear aligners. Smile transformations that look completely natural.

Family Dentistry

From your toddler's first visit to your parents' regular maintenance — we care for every generation with patience and warmth.

Restorative Care

Crowns, bridges, and tooth-colored fillings that restore function beautifully. We fix what's broken and make it look like it never was.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-feeling tooth replacement that looks and functions exactly like your original teeth — for life.

Emergency Dentistry

Dental emergencies don't wait for business hours. Same-day care for toothaches, broken teeth, and urgent needs.

About the Practice

Dentistry built around people, not procedures.

Dr. Charles Ferzli opened Smiles of Cary because he believed patients deserved better — not just technically excellent care, but a practice where they actually felt comfortable walking in the door.

Over 15 years later, that philosophy still drives every appointment. No upselling. No rushing. Just honest, thorough care delivered by a team that knows your name and remembers your story.
