About Us

The kind of care that feels like family.

At Aversboro Road Child Care Center, we believe every child deserves an environment where they feel safe, seen, and genuinely cared for. Our certified staff creates a warm, structured space where children thrive — not just grow.

Located at 1701 Aversboro Road in Garner, we've earned a 4-Star North Carolina childcare license — the state's highest quality recognition. That matters to us, because it reflects how seriously we take our responsibility to your family.

  • Certified childcare personnel — background-checked

Our Programs

Programs for every stage.

From your newest addition to your school-age child — we have structured, nurturing programs designed for every developmental stage.

6 Weeks – 12 Months

Infant Care

Individual attention, consistent routines, and a warm environment designed for your youngest child's earliest development.

1 – 2 Years

Toddler Program

Structured play, language development, and social skills — guided by patient, experienced staff in a safe, stimulating space.

3 – 5 Years

Preschool

School readiness, early literacy, numbers, and social foundations — preparing your child with the skills and confidence for kindergarten.

School Age

Before & After School

Reliable morning drop-off and afternoon pickup care for school-age children, with homework support and structured activities.

Year-Round

Track-Out Programs

Enrichment activities and safe supervision during school track-out periods, so your work schedule doesn't miss a beat.

All Ages

Academic Enrichment
