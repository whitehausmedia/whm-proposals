About Little Thinkers

Since 2008, Little Thinkers has been the trusted choice for early childhood education in Burlington. Our play-based learning philosophy ensures every child develops confidence, curiosity, and lifelong love of learning.

Play-Based Learning

Intentional exploration through play that builds critical thinking and social skills.

Two Locations

2485 Moran St & 731 N Fisher St, both serving Burlington families with excellence.

Locally Owned

Rooted in our community, invested in your child's growth and family's success.
