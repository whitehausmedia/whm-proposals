Who We Are

Kids Country Day Care is a trusted, family-owned childcare provider serving Smithfield, Clayton, and the surrounding Johnston County area. Pam Ryan leads a team dedicated to personalized, quality care for every child.

With two locations offering flexibility and convenience, we provide infant care, toddler programs, preschool, pre-K, before/after school, and early learning opportunities that grow with your child.

Every child receives individualized attention in a safe, supportive environment where learning and laughter go hand in hand.
