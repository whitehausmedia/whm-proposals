Programs by age

A classroom built for every stage — from bottles to backpacks.

From the first smiles of infancy to the confident kindness of a school-age child, every age group is led by educators who love children and understand exactly what that year of life needs.

Infants · 6 wks – 1 yr

Cradle Room

Gentle routines, soft music, and a partnership with parents built on daily communication and trust.

Toddlers · 1 – 2 yrs

Little Steps

Sensory play, early words, and first friendships in a safe, ever-curious classroom filled with laughter.

Preschool · 3 – 5 yrs

Believers Class

Letters, numbers, Bible stories, and Kindergarten-ready habits woven into purposeful play every day.

School-Age · 5 – 12 yrs

After-School Care

Homework help, Christian enrichment, and free play for kids growing through the elementary years.

Why Clayton & Garner families choose us

Faithful care, family-run, every day.

Rooted in Christian values

Faith isn't an add-on at Little Believer's — it's woven into the songs we sing, the stories we read, and the kindness we teach. Every child leaves here loved and known.

Family-run & community-rooted

Founded and led by the Brooks family, with two local directors who know every parent by name. When something matters, you reach a real person — not a call center.

Small ratios, credentialed teachers

Every classroom is staffed with certified educators who stay with us for years. Your child grows up knowing their teacher — and being known in return.
