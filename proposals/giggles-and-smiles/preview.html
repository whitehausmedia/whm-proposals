Our Approach

Giggles and Smiles Playschool specializes in loving, attentive care for infants and toddlers ages 0-2. As a small family child care home, we provide the personalized attention every young child needs during these critical developmental years.

  • Individual care plans tailored to each child
  • Safe, nurturing home-like environment
  • State-licensed and fully insured
  • Small group sizes for maximum attention
  • Regular communication with parents
