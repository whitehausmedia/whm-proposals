Our Programs

Infant Care

6 weeks to 12 months. Responsive, individualized care with secure attachments, developmental milestones, and family communication.

Toddler Care

12 to 24 months. Language development, exploration-based learning, and social skill foundations in a safe, nurturing environment.

Early Preschool

Ages 2-3. Pre-academic learning, cooperative play, and emergent literacy through play-based curriculum.

Pre-K (4-5)

Ages 4-5. Kindergarten readiness, advanced academics, and school transition support with certified instructors.

Nutrition Program

On-site kitchen-prepared meals and snacks. Balanced nutrition for growing bodies, with allergy accommodations.
