What We Found

Three things costing you
new patients

We reviewed zentriangledentistry.com directly. Here is what we found — no assumptions, just what is live on the site today.

No real-time booking — a contact form is not a scheduler
When a patient clicks "Book an Appointment," they get a form and a wait. Real-time scheduling lets them pick a time, get a confirmation, and show up — no phone tag, no friction, no opportunity for them to choose another practice while waiting to hear back from you.
Testimonials section with no testimonials
The site has a dedicated testimonials section, but it is empty — no review text, no patient names, no social proof. For a healthcare practice, peer reviews are one of the strongest conversion signals there is. A blank section signals to visitors that no one has vouched for you.
No schema markup — invisible in local dental searches
Google uses structured data (schema markup) to identify dental practices, display star ratings, show hours, and surface "dentist near me" results. zentriangledentistry.com has none of it, which means local competitors with proper schema are capturing the visibility your practice should own.
