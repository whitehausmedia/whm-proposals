Our Story

Where the Mediterranean
meets the Triangle.

Mediterranean cuisine is more than a menu — it's a way of eating that centers on freshness, generosity, and the pleasure of a shared table. At Nils, every dish begins with ingredients sourced for flavor first: bright herbs, slow-cooked proteins, hand-pressed olive oil, and spices that take you somewhere warmer the moment they hit the pan.

Our Fuquay-Varina location brings that same philosophy here — to a community that appreciates good food and the kind of restaurant worth driving across town for. Whether you're here for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion, we're glad you found us.
