A Note From Our Director

Built on trust, run with heart.

Fuquay-Varina has grown quickly, and we've grown right alongside it. What hasn't changed in over a decade is the thing that matters most: the reason we opened South Wake Preschool & Academy in the first place. We believe every family in this community deserves an early childhood program that feels like an extension of their home — warm, consistent, and deeply invested in their child's wellbeing.

We're not a franchise. We don't answer to a corporate office. Every decision we make here is made with the families of Fuquay-Varina in mind. That's been true since we started, and it will stay true as we keep growing together.

— Monica Escamilla, Founder & Director
