About Peter

A financial advisor who actually knows your name.

Peter Richon founded Richon Planning to serve the Triangle community the way he'd want to be served: with honesty, clarity, and genuine care for the outcome. After years in the financial industry, he launched an independent practice specifically to remove the conflicts of interest that come with working for a large firm — so every conversation starts from the same place: what is actually best for you.

Peter is a longtime resident of the Fuquay-Varina area, and many of his clients are neighbors, referrals from neighbors, and business owners in the same community. That's not a coincidence — it's the way he prefers to work.
