Our Programs

Young Preschool

Ages 18-30 months. Sensory exploration, language development, and secure attachment in a warm, nurturing setting.

Preschool

Ages 2-3 years. Social skills, creative play, early literacy, and independence building through hands-on learning.

Pre-K

Ages 3-4 years. Academic readiness, collaborative learning, and confidence building as children prepare for elementary.

Transitional Kindergarten

Ages 4-5 years. A bridge year combining structured academics with play-based learning for optimal development.

Kindergarten

Ages 5-6 years. Full-day program with rigorous academics, enrichment activities, and social-emotional development.
