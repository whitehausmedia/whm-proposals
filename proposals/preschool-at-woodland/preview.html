A Place Where Children Thrive

Preschool at Woodland is built on the foundation of faith, community, and compassionate education. We believe every child deserves a nurturing environment where they can explore, learn, and grow into confident, curious learners.

Developmental Milestones: Programs designed around age-appropriate activities
Small Class Sizes: More one-on-one attention and personalized care
Community Connection: Regular parent involvement and events
Safe, Engaging Space: Purpose-built facilities and outdoor play areas

Our Programs

2-Year-Olds

Sensory-rich exploration with music, movement, and early social development in a safe, language-rich environment.

3-Year-Olds

Growing independence through structured play, pre-academic activities, and small group learning experiences.

4-5 Year-Olds
