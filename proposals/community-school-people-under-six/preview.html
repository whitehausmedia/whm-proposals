A School Built on Community

For more than 50 years, Community School for People Under Six has been a pillar of Carrboro's family life. We believe children learn best in communities of trust, play, and creative exploration—where families and educators work together.

Creative Curriculum: Art, music, nature, and hands-on learning drive development
Community Partnership: Families are partners, not bystanders, in their child's growth
Play-Based Learning: Child-led exploration at the heart of all activities
Whole-Child Focus: Development spans cognitive, social, emotional, and physical domains

Programs for Every Stage

Infant Care

Nurturing, responsive care for newborns and infants (ages 0–12 months) in a loving, language-rich environment.

Toddler Community

Exploration, play, and early learning for ages 1–3 with art, music, and outdoor discovery integrated daily.

Preschool

Advanced creative curriculum for ages 3–5, with emphasis on problem-solving, social skills, and joy in learning.
