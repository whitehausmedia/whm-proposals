Our Mission

Higher Calling Childcare Center combines world-class early childhood education with faith-based values. We create a safe, loving environment where children learn, grow, and develop lasting foundations for success.

  • Comprehensive curriculum aligned with developmental milestones
  • Small class ratios for personalized attention
  • Daily spiritual guidance and character building
  • Nutritious meals and snacks prepared fresh
  • Parent communication and involvement
