Meet Ms. Houston

Early learning that earns the trust of parents and the attention of children.

Briana Ambr'e Houston built Learning with Ms. Houston on a simple belief: young children deserve content that respects their intelligence, mirrors their world, and teaches them something real.

A state-certified educator with a Master's in Education, Briana has taught across public, charter, and Montessori classrooms, founded a micro-school, and continues to homeschool her own children. Every song, printable, and lesson on this site is made by an educator who still teaches, still writes curriculum, and still watches what actually lands with children.

Her animated sing-alongs and interactive lessons now reach nearly one in five U.S. parents with young children, with a focus on diverse representation and characters that look like the scholars watching them. This is more than a channel. It is a curriculum layer for the families and classrooms who have been waiting for it.
