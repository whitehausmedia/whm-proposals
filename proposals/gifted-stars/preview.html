Why Choose Gifted Stars

STEM Innovation

We integrate science, technology, engineering & math into every day—preparing children for a tech-forward world while keeping play at the center.

Holistic Approach

STEM + wellness + creativity. We nurture the whole child—mind, body, and spirit—through enrichment programs like yoga, cooking, and sign language.

Multi-Campus Excellence

Two thriving locations in the Triangle, each with the same commitment to hands-on learning, organic nutrition, and individualized care.

Learning by Doing

Every child learns differently. We believe concrete, hands-on activities build confidence, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Organic & Healthy

We serve organic meals and snacks—nutrition that fuels growing brains and supports healthy development every single day.

Experienced Team

Our educators are passionate, trained, and committed to creating a safe, loving, stimulating environment where every child thrives.
