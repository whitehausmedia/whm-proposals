The Opportunity

Four gaps in your current digital presence—and four reasons a modern website moves the needle.

Outdated Design

Your current site hasn't been refreshed in several years. Parents searching for childcare expect modern, clean design. A dated look signals outdated practices—even if that's not true.

No Mobile Optimization

80% of childcare searches happen on mobile devices. Your site isn't optimized for phone users, meaning prospects bounce before learning what Appletree offers.

Missing Enrollment Tool

Interested families have no way to begin enrollment online. They must call or email, creating friction and friction kills conversions. You're leaving opportunities on the table.

Weak Search Visibility

Your site doesn't rank well for local searches ("daycare near me," "childcare Raleigh"). Families looking for you may never find you. SEO-optimized content changes that.

The Solution

A custom website purpose-built for Appletree, with every feature designed to convert families into enrolled students.

✦

Responsive Design

Beautiful on all devices—desktop, tablet, mobile. Built for speed and optimized for user experience.

✦

Program Showcase

Dedicated sections for Infant Care, Toddler, Preschool, Pre-K, Before & After School, and Summer Camp with clear age ranges and descriptions.

✦

Online Enrollment Form

Fully integrated contact form allowing families to start the enrollment process directly. Leads go straight to your inbox.

✦

Location & Hours

Clear display of all four Raleigh centers with addresses, directions, phone numbers, and hours. No confusion, no lost leads.

✦

SEO Optimization

Keyword-optimized content, meta tags, and structure so families searching "childcare Raleigh" find Appletree.

✦
