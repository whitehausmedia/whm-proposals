About Children's Campus

Trusted by Triangle Families

For more than 30 years, Children's Campus has been the choice of families seeking quality, nurturing childcare across the Triangle. With three convenient locations serving Durham and Chapel Hill, we provide comprehensive programs from infant care through Pre-K.

Our team of experienced educators creates safe, stimulating environments where children thrive developmentally and emotionally. We combine structured learning with play-based discovery to support each child's unique growth journey.

🏫

Our Programs

Infant Care

Gentle, attentive care for newborns through 12 months, with structured routines supporting healthy development and parent communication.

Toddler Program

Exploration-based learning for ages 1-3, developing motor skills, language, and social growth through safe, engaging activities.

Preschool

Structured curriculum for ages 3-4, introducing pre-academic skills, creativity, and social development through play and learning.

Pre-K Program

Comprehensive preparation for kindergarten, building academic foundations, independence, and critical thinking skills.

Full-Day Care

Extended hours (Monday-Friday) providing continuity and flexibility for working families across all age groups.

Enrichment Activities

Art, music, physical education, and outdoor play integrated throughout the day to support holistic child development.
