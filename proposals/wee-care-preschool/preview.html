About Wee Care

A place of learning, faith, and belonging.

Wee Care Preschool has been a trusted part of the Wake Forest Baptist Church community for decades — offering preschool education grounded in Christian values, play-based learning, and genuine care for every child.

Under the direction of Kennie Schwier, our teachers bring experience, warmth, and a shared mission: to help your child build a strong foundation of social skills, independence, and love of learning.

  • Creative Curriculum — research-based, play-centered approach
  • All staff background-checked and CPR certified
  • Move & Groove, Chapel, and Library special programs
  • Now enrolling 2s, 3s, and 4s for 2026–2027
