Our Mission

Nurturing every child's potential

Heavenly Haven provides a nurturing environment that fosters and develops children's cognitive, physical, social, and emotional abilities. Our devoted staff view their positions as callings, not just jobs — preparing every child for school and life success.

Whole-Child Development

Academic readiness, social skills, and emotional confidence — developed together through intentional, play-based learning.

Values-Aligned Education

A faith-centered approach that gives families confidence their children are growing in character as much as in knowledge.

On-Site Therapy Services

Speech, occupational, physical, and behavioral therapy available in-house — so children get the support they need without leaving the center.

Programs

Age-appropriate learning
at every stage

From first steps to school readiness, our Creative Curriculum framework meets your child exactly where they are — building confidence, curiosity, and critical thinking skills.

6 Weeks – 1 Year

Infant Care

Loving, responsive care from CPR and First Aid certified staff. A warm, safe environment where your baby's earliest milestones are celebrated and supported.

1 – 2 Years

Toddler Program

Exploration-based learning through play, movement, and sensory activities. Building language, motor skills, and social connections during these foundational years.

2 – 5 Years

Preschool

The Creative Curriculum comes alive through structured play, outdoor activities, and hands-on learning — building critical thinking and school readiness every day.

Age 4 • Free Program

NC Pre-K

A federally funded, year-round program bridging preschool and kindergarten. Six and a half hours daily of literacy, math, science, music, and art instruction.

Up to 12 Years

Before & After School

Safe, supportive care before and after the school day with homework help, enrichment activities, and time to just be a kid with friends.
