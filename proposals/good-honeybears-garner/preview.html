Our Story

Twenty-Five Years of Caring for Garner's Children

Good-HoneyBears Child Development has proudly served the Garner community for over 25 years. What started as a single center has grown to multiple locations — all united by the same philosophy: that every child has the ability to learn, and that our responsibility is to help them develop to their full potential.

We care for children from newborn through age ten across our Garner locations, offering a structured, nurturing environment that parents in the community have trusted across generations of families.

When you bring your child to Good-HoneyBears, you're not enrolling in a franchise. You're joining a Garner community institution that has been here longer than most of its competition.
