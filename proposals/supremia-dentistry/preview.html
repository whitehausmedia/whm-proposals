What We Offer

Comprehensive care for every stage of life.

From your child's first visit to advanced cosmetic work, we bring expert skill and a gentle touch to every appointment.

Preventive Care

Regular cleanings, digital X-rays, and oral health exams that catch problems early and keep your smile healthy year after year.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, and Invisalign clear aligners — smile enhancements crafted with precision and artistry.

Restorative Dentistry

Tooth-colored fillings, crowns, bridges, and dental implants that restore function and blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.

Family Dentistry

Care for every member of your household in one practice — from children's first exams to adult maintenance and everything between.

Emergency Dentistry

Same-day emergency appointments for toothaches, broken teeth, and urgent dental needs. We're here when you need us most.

New Patient Exams

A thorough first visit that includes a full health review, digital X-rays, and a personalized treatment plan — no rush, ever.

About Dr. Suh

Precision care rooted in genuine compassion.

Dr. Edmond Suh founded Supremia Dentistry with a single purpose: to deliver the kind of dental care he'd want for his own family. That means taking time, explaining every step, and never rushing through an appointment.

With advanced training in cosmetic and restorative dentistry and a commitment to staying current with the latest clinical techniques, Dr. Suh brings expertise and empathy in equal measure to every patient he sees.
