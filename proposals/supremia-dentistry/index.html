Where the opportunity lives

Four things holding Supremia Dentistry back online.

After reviewing your current site and digital footprint, here is what we found — and why it's costing you new patients.

01

No Online Booking — Patients Move On

Your current site has no integrated appointment booking. Patients who find you at 9pm or on the weekend — exactly when people decide they need a dentist — have no way to act on that impulse. A simple booking flow captures those patients instead of sending them to a competitor who has one. Every day without it is a day patients choose someone else.

02

Weak Trust Signals Across the Board

Reviews, credentials, and before-and-after photography are the three things that make a new dental patient feel safe. Your current site surfaces none of them prominently. No Google rating in the hero, no testimonials section, no visible credentials for Dr. Suh. Patients comparing two dentists online will choose the one who makes them feel more confident — and right now, that's not you.

03

A Generic Design That Undersells the Practice

Supremia Dentistry is a premium practice, but the current website looks like a free template. The color palette, stock images, and default layout don't signal the quality of care that's actually delivered in your office. First impressions happen in under three seconds online. A design that matches the premium experience you provide earns patient trust before they've ever met you.

04

Missing Local SEO Foundation

Wake Forest is a fast-growing community, and new residents are actively searching for a dentist right now. Your current site lacks the local SEO fundamentals — proper schema markup, location-specific page titles, Google Business alignment — that would put Supremia Dentistry at the top of those searches. A clean modern build fixes this at the foundation level, not as an afterthought.
