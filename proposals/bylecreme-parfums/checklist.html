Prepared For ByLeCreme

This workbook is designed to help you walk through your current site with fresh eyes. Our goal is not to identify what is wrong, but to give you space to imagine what could be refined, elevated, or reimagined as we prepare to build.

There are no right or wrong answers. Some sections may not apply, and some may inspire ideas we have not yet discussed. Capture everything that comes to mind. The more clarity we have now, the more intentional the first draft becomes.

A Note From Our Team

We recommend reviewing bylecreme.com on both desktop and mobile as you complete this checklist. Take your time. This is the quietest part of the process, and often the most important.
