Why families choose us

An approach you won't find everywhere.

Most preschools are good. Bridges and Beyond is designed for families who want intentional — a program that sees their child as an individual, not a number.

8:1 Child-to-Teacher Ratio
Only 8 children per class. This isn't a policy — it's our foundation. Fewer children means more attention, faster recognition of needs, and a calmer environment where every child can thrive.
Inclusive by Design
Our classrooms welcome children with special needs alongside their peers. Inclusion isn't accommodation — it's our core belief that every child belongs and grows together.
Developmental Focus, Ages 2–5
Every activity, routine, and interaction is designed around the developmental milestones that matter at this age. Play is purposeful. Learning is joyful. Growth is intentional.

About Bridges and Beyond

Built by an educator who believes every child deserves more.

Bridges and Beyond Preschool was founded on a simple conviction: that the early years are too important to spend in an overcrowded classroom. Debi Lilienthal, Director and Owner, built this school around the model she wished every family could access — small, intentional, inclusive, and genuinely caring.

Located in Wake Forest, NC, our school serves families across the Triangle who are looking for something different: a program where their child isn't lost in a crowd, where special needs aren't an afterthought, and where teachers have the time and space to truly know each child.

"We keep our classes small because we believe every child deserves to be known — not just managed. Eight to one isn't just a number. It's a commitment."

— Debi Lilienthal, Director & Owner
