The Method

What is
Lagree?

Lagree is a science-backed training method developed by Sebastien Lagree — combining the principles of Pilates, strength training, and endurance into a single, continuous 50-minute session on the Megaformer machine.

Unlike traditional Pilates, Lagree keeps your muscles under constant tension through slow, deliberate movements — which means more muscle fiber recruitment, more metabolic demand, and faster visible results. It's not cardio. It's not weightlifting. It's both — without the impact.

⚡

High-intensity, low-impact

Your heart rate stays elevated throughout, but there's zero jumping, pounding, or joint stress. Sustainable for life.

🎯

Slow is the secret

Movements take 10–14 seconds each way. That time under tension is what makes Lagree dramatically more effective than faster methods.

⚖

Infinitely modifiable

Every exercise adjusts for your fitness level via spring tension. Beginners and athletes work side by side in the same class.

The Results

Three reasons Lagree works
when everything else plateaus.

01

Full body in every single session

Every class at Elite Core hits every major muscle group — core, glutes, legs, arms, back — in 50 minutes. No split days. No wasted sessions. Just consistent, full-body activation that produces visible changes in 8 to 12 sessions.

02

Low-impact means you can actually keep going

Traditional HIIT destroys your joints and requires rest days. Lagree's controlled, spring-loaded movements create intense muscular work with zero concussive force — which means you can train 3 to 5 times a week without burning out.

03

Modify for any level, every time

Whether you're recovering from an injury, completely new to fitness, or a trained athlete looking for a new challenge — Lagree scales to you. Our instructors guide every spring tension change in real time.
