Our Story

Indigo Montessori School has served the Durham community since 2001, providing a nurturing environment where children flourish academically and emotionally.

A Prepared Community

Located near Duke East Campus and the vibrant Brightleaf Square, Indigo Montessori offers families a convenient, welcoming space where their children can thrive. Our classrooms are thoughtfully designed to inspire discovery, respect, and independent thought.

We believe that every child is a unique individual with their own pace of development. Our role is to guide, not direct—to offer the tools for learning while nurturing the whole child: mind, heart, and hands.

The Montessori Method

Developed by Dr. Maria Montessori over a century ago, the Montessori approach is rooted in respect for the child's natural development. Children learn through hands-on, self-directed exploration in a carefully prepared environment.

Our practice honors three core principles:

  • Respect for the Child: Each child develops at their own pace, guided by intrinsic motivation rather than external rewards or punishment.
  • Prepared Environment: Classrooms are thoughtfully organized with age-appropriate materials that invite exploration and discovery.
  • Guided Participation: Teachers observe, guide, and facilitate—fostering independence, concentration, and a genuine love of learning.
