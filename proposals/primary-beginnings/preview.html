Why Families Choose Primary Beginnings

Play-Based Philosophy

Research shows children learn best through play. Our curriculum is built on this foundation, balancing exploration with guided learning.

Small Class Sizes

We maintain low student-to-teacher ratios, ensuring every child receives individualized attention and support throughout their day.

Experienced Staff

Our team brings decades of early childhood expertise. Every educator is certified and passionate about child development.

5-Star Accreditation

We've earned North Carolina's highest accreditation status, demonstrating our commitment to excellence and quality care.

Safe & Secure

State-of-the-art facilities across all three locations, with secure entry systems, updated equipment, and rigorous health protocols.

Community Focus

We foster strong connections between families and staff, creating a supportive community that extends beyond classroom walls.

Our Programs

Infant Care

Nurturing care for our youngest learners (6 weeks - 12 months), with structured routines, sensory exploration, and loving guidance.

Toddler Care

Ages 1-2: Hands-on learning, language development, and social skills through music, art, and interactive play.

Preschool

Ages 2-3: Creative problem-solving, early academics, and social-emotional development in a play-based setting.

Pre-K

Ages 3-5: School readiness skills combined with discovery learning to build confidence and curiosity.

Before & After School

Extended hours for school-age children, with homework support and enrichment activities in a safe, welcoming environment.

Extracurriculars

Enrichment classes in music, art, movement, and STEM—designed to inspire and develop well-rounded learners.
