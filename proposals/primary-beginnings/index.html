The Opportunity

Your current web presence isn't matching the quality of your on-ground experience.

Outdated Design & Experience

Your current site uses generic templates and doesn't reflect the premium nature of your programs or your 30+ year legacy. Modern families expect polished, intentional design.

Limited Mobile Experience

With 60%+ of child care searches happening on mobile, your site struggles on phones. This is costing you leads who can't navigate or don't see enrollment options clearly.

No Enrollment System Online

Families have to call or fill out paper forms. This friction costs you interested prospects who are ready to move forward but take the path of least resistance elsewhere.

Weak Local SEO Visibility

You're not ranking for high-intent keywords like "play-based preschool Raleigh" or "5-star child care Falls of Neuse." Families aren't finding you easily in search.

Our Solution

A premium, custom-built website that converts families and reflects the excellence you deliver every day.

✦ Custom Design & Brand Expression

A premium, fully custom site built from scratch (not a template) that uses your brand colors and communicates the professionalism and care that define your center.

✦ Fully Responsive Mobile Experience

Flawless functionality on phones, tablets, and desktops. Enrollment, program details, and contact info all optimized for mobile-first families.

✦ Online Enrollment Inquiry Form

Families can express interest, provide basic information, and request a tour directly from the site. Inquiries delivered to your email in real-time.

✦ Program & Location Showcase

Dedicated pages for each program (Infant through Pre-K, Before & After School, Extracurriculars) and all three locations, with rich details and enrollment CTAs.

Built-in technical SEO, structured data for child care search engines, and strategic keyword optimization to help families find you on Google.
