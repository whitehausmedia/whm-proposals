Full Service Restoration

Everything your log home needs.

From the first inspection to the final coat — every step handled with the same attention to detail.

Repair & Restoration

Structural repairs, log rot removal, corner replacement, and full restoration bringing your cabin back to its original strength.

Chinking & Caulking

Proper sealing that locks out moisture, insects, and air infiltration while preserving the authentic character of the logs.

Pressure Washing

Professional cleaning and media blast stripping that removes years of weathering, mold, and old finish — the essential first step.

Stain Application

Exterior and interior stain applied with precision — protecting the wood for years while enhancing its natural grain and beauty.

pH & Moisture Testing

Diagnostic testing that identifies hidden moisture problems and pH imbalances before they become costly structural issues.

Maintenance Programs

Annual and seasonal maintenance plans that protect your investment long after the restoration is complete.

Insect Prevention

Treatment protecting log structures from wood-boring insects and termites that can compromise structural integrity over time.

Deck Sealers & Stains

Deck restoration and sealing that extends the life of your outdoor spaces and keeps them looking their best season after season.
