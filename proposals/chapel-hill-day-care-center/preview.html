Our Story

Chapel Hill Day Care Center is built on the principle that early childhood is sacred. We believe children thrive when they develop deep, consistent relationships with their caregivers — which is why our continuity of care model keeps children and teachers together through their most critical developmental years.

Located in the heart of Southern Village with easy access to the Park & Ride and playground, we offer an evidence-based, play-based curriculum in a warm, nurturing environment. Our NAEYC accreditation reflects our commitment to the highest standards in early education.

Why Families Choose Chapel Hill Day Care

NAEYC Accreditation

We meet rigorous national standards for quality. Your child benefits from best practices in early education and evidence-based curriculum.

Continuity of Care

Children and teachers stay together from 6 weeks through age 3. Deep relationships build secure attachments and emotional resilience.

Exceptional Ratios

Our low teacher-to-child ratios (1:3 to 1:6) ensure each child receives individualized attention and care.
